Explore the Features

Mini Kraken is a bot for Discord that offers a wide variety of functions to help you on your tabletop RPG journey. Roll dice, manage initiatives, create characters, use name generators, and much more. Check here just a small sample of everything the Mini Kraken can do for you!

Life calculation

Don't waste time calculating your character's health manually. With the /edit life command, the Mini Kraken does the math for you, allowing you to focus on playing.

Dice roll

Inanimalia Fortuna has an advanced dice-rolling system, allowing several combinations with the /d command. Don't waste any more time doing complex calculations, let Mini Kraken take care of it for you!

Smart sheets

Easily organize character information with Smart Sheets. With simple commands such as /sheet it is possible to create and edit character sheets directly in the bot, making your table more agile and efficient.

Customization

Select from the many available RPG systems to further customize your campaign. With options like Paranormal Order, Dungeons & Dragons, Carbon2185 and Tormenta, and if that's not enough, create your own system.

Find Players

Easily find players for your RPG table using the Fortune Campaign Disclosure system. With the /send campaign command,
Don't waste any more time looking for players, let Fortune help you!

Gerenators

A variety of name and letter generators to help with character creation. With the /generate name and /deck commands, you can generate random names, tarot cards or decks to enrich your narrative.

Economy

An extensive Economy system called KC (Kraken Coins) that can be obtained in several ways, and will be useful for functions that are still in development.

Book of Spells

Find spells and rituals in your spellbook more easily with our spellbook that lets you search on the fly!
